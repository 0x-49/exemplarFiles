### Comprehensive Text Description of the Navan Terms of Service Page

The **Navan Terms of Service (ToS) page** is a critical legal document that outlines the rules, responsibilities, and guidelines governing the use of Navan's platform and services. This page is designed to provide clarity and transparency to users, ensuring they understand their rights and obligations when engaging with Navan. Below is a detailed description of the page's structure, content, themes, and design elements.

---

#### **1. Page Layout and Structure**
The Terms of Service page is structured to ensure readability and accessibility, with a clear hierarchy of information. The layout is divided into the following sections:

- **Header**: 
  - Contains the Navan logo and a link to return to the homepage.
  - A simple, clean design with a white background and dark text for contrast.
  - Includes a breadcrumb navigation trail (e.g., Home > Legal > Terms of Service) for easy navigation.

- **Main Content Area**:
  - The core of the page is a long-form text document, divided into sections and subsections for easy reference.
  - Each section is clearly labeled with headings and subheadings, using a consistent typography style (e.g., bold, larger font sizes for headings).
  - The text is left-aligned, with ample white space to improve readability.

- **Footer**:
  - Includes links to other legal documents (e.g., Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy, Security).
  - Provides contact information for legal inquiries.
  - Features a copyright notice and links to Navan's social media channels.

---

#### **2. Typography and Color Scheme**
- **Typography**:
  - **Headings**: Sans-serif font (e.g., Arial or Helvetica) in bold, with a larger font size (e.g., 24px for main headings, 18px for subheadings).
  - **Body Text**: Sans-serif font in a standard size (e.g., 16px) with a line height of 1.5 for readability.

- **Color Scheme**:
  - **Background**: White (#FFFFFF) for the main content area, ensuring high contrast with the text.
  - **Text**: Dark gray (#333333) for body text, with black (#000000) for headings.

#### **3. Content Sections**
The Terms of Service document is divided into logical sections, each addressing a specific aspect of the agreement. Below is a breakdown of the key sections:

##### **1. Introduction**
- **Purpose**: Explains the purpose of the document and its applicability to users.

- **Data Collection**: Explains what data Navan collects and how it is used.
