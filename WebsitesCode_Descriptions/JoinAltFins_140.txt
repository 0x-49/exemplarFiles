### Comprehensive Description of the altFINS Portfolio Management Page

The **Portfolio Management** page on the altFINS platform is designed to be a centralized hub for users to track, analyze, and optimize their cryptocurrency investments. ### **Page Overview**
The **Portfolio Management** page is a visually rich and data-driven interface that allows users to monitor their cryptocurrency holdings, analyze performance, and make informed decisions. The page is divided into several key sections, each serving a specific purpose:

1. **Portfolio Summary**
2. **Asset Allocation**
3. **Performance Metrics**
4. **Trade History**
5. **Automated Coin Tracking**
6. **Customizable Watchlists**
7. **Interactive Charts**

9. **Educational Resources**
10. **Footer and Navigation**

---

### **1. Portfolio Summary**
The **Portfolio Summary** section is the first thing users see when they land on the page. It provides a high-level overview of their cryptocurrency holdings.

- **Key Features:**
  - **Total Portfolio Value:** Displays the current value of the user’s portfolio in USD or their preferred currency.
  - **24h Change:** Shows the percentage change in the portfolio’s value over the last 24 hours.
  - **All-Time Profit/Loss:** Highlights the overall profit or loss since the portfolio’s inception.
  - **Top Performers:** Lists the top 3 performing assets in the portfolio.
  - **Worst Performers:** Lists the bottom 3 performing assets in the portfolio.

- **Design Elements:**
  - **Color Scheme:** Green for positive changes, red for negative changes, and neutral tones for static data.
  - **Typography:** Bold, large fonts for key metrics (e.g., Total Portfolio Value) and smaller, readable fonts for secondary data.
  - **Visuals:** Icons representing each cryptocurrency, with tooltips for additional details.

### **2. Asset Allocation**
The **Asset Allocation** section provides a visual representation of how the user’s portfolio is distributed across different cryptocurrencies.

- **Key Features:**
  - **Pie Chart:** A dynamic pie chart that breaks down the portfolio by asset type.
  - **Table View:** A detailed table showing the percentage allocation, current value, and quantity of each asset.
  - **Filter Options:** Users can filter by asset type, market cap, or performance.

- **Design Elements:**
  - **Color Scheme:** Vibrant, distinct colors for each cryptocurrency to ensure clarity.
  - **Interactivity:** Hover effects on the pie chart to display additional details (e.g., asset name, percentage).
  - **Responsive Design:** The pie chart and table adjust seamlessly for mobile and desktop views.

---

### **3. Performance Metrics**
This section offers in-depth analytics on the portfolio’s performance over time.

- **Key Features:**
  - **Historical Performance Graph:** A line graph showing the portfolio’s value over days, weeks, or months.
  - **Risk Metrics:** Includes metrics like Sharpe Ratio, Volatility, and Maximum Drawdown.
  - **Benchmark Comparison:** Allows users to compare their portfolio’s performance against a benchmark (e.g., Bitcoin, Ethereum, or a custom index).

- **Design Elements:**
  - **Color Scheme:** Blue for the portfolio’s performance line and gray for benchmarks.
  - **Tooltips:** Interactive tooltips on the graph to display exact values at specific points in time.
  - **Export Options:** Users can export performance data as a CSV or PDF.

---

### **4. Trade History**
The **Trade History** section provides a detailed log of all transactions made within the portfolio.

- **Key Features:**
  - **Transaction Table:** Lists all buy/sell transactions with details like date, asset, quantity, price, and fees.
  - **Filter and Sort Options:** Users can filter by date range, asset, or transaction type (buy/sell).
  - **Profit/Loss Calculation:** Automatically calculates the profit or loss for each transaction.

- **Design Elements:**
  - **Color Scheme:** Green for profitable trades, red for losing trades, and neutral tones for pending or break-even trades.
  - **Pagination:** The table is paginated for easy navigation, with options to show 10, 25, or 50 entries per page.
  - **Search Bar:** A search bar allows users to quickly find specific transactions.
