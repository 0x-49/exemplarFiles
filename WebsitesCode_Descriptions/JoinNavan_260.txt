### Comprehensive Description of the Navan Sustainability Page

The **Navan Sustainability Page** (https://www.joinnavan.com/sustainability) is a dedicated section of the Navan website that highlights the company’s commitment to environmental responsibility, sustainable business practices, and innovative solutions for reducing the carbon footprint of corporate travel and expense management. This page is designed to appeal to environmentally conscious businesses, travel managers, finance teams, and executives who prioritize sustainability in their operations. Below is a detailed breakdown of the page’s structure, content, themes, colors, components, and features.

---

1. **Hero Section**
2. **Sustainability Commitment Overview**

4. **Carbon Emissions Tracking**

10. **Footer Section**
