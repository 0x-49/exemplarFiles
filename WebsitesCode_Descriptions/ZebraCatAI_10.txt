### Comprehensive Description of the Zebracat Pricing Page

The **Zebracat Pricing Page** is designed to provide a clear, concise, and visually appealing breakdown of the platform's subscription plans, features, and benefits. The page is structured to guide users through the available options, helping them make informed decisions based on their needs. Below is a detailed description of all text elements, features, themes, colors, components, and interactive elements that make up the page.

---

#### **1. Hero Section**
The hero section is the first thing users see when they land on the pricing page. It is designed to grab attention and provide an immediate overview of what Zebracat offers.

- **Headline:**  
  **"Simple, Transparent Pricing for Every Creator"**  
  This bold, attention-grabbing headline emphasizes simplicity and transparency, key values for users evaluating pricing options.

- **Subheadline:**  
  **"Choose the plan that fits your needs. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, Zebracat has you covered."**  
  This subheadline reassures users that there is a plan for everyone, regardless of their skill level or budget.

- **Call-to-Action (CTA) Buttons:**  
  Two primary CTA buttons are prominently displayed:  
  - **"Start with Free Plan"** (in a secondary color, e.g., light gray or white)  
  - **"Explore Paid Plans"** (in a vibrant primary color, e.g., Zebracat's brand blue or green)  
  These buttons are designed to guide users toward the next step, whether they want to start for free or explore premium options.

- **Visual Elements:**  
  A dynamic background featuring subtle animations of Zebracat's AI-generated video scenes, showcasing the platform's capabilities. This reinforces the value proposition visually.

---

#### **2. Pricing Plans Comparison**
The core of the page is the **Pricing Plans Comparison** section, which provides a detailed breakdown of the three main plans: **Free**, **Cat Mode**, and **Super Cat**.

- **Plan Cards:**  
  Each plan is presented in a clean, card-based layout with a distinct color scheme to differentiate them:  
  - **Free Plan:** Light blue or gray background, symbolizing accessibility and simplicity.  
  - **Cat Mode:** Vibrant orange or yellow, representing energy and premium features.  
  - **Super Cat:** Deep blue or purple, conveying exclusivity and advanced capabilities.

  Each card includes:  
  - **Plan Name:** Bold and prominently displayed at the top.  
  - **Price:** Large, bold font with a clear breakdown (e.g., "$0/month" for Free, "$29/month" for Cat Mode, "$99/month" for Super Cat).  
  - **Billing Options:** A toggle switch allowing users to view pricing as **Monthly** or **Annually** (with discounts for annual subscriptions).  
  - **Key Features:** A bullet-point list of the most important features for each plan, such as:  
    - **Free Plan:**  
      - 5 credits/week  
      - 720p video export  
      - Watermarked videos  
      - Basic AI voices and visuals  
    - **Cat Mode:**  
      - 120 credits/month  
      - 1080p video export  
      - No watermarks  
      - Ultra-realistic AI voices  
      - Premium AI visuals  
    - **Super Cat:**  
      - 300 credits/month  
      - 1080p video export  
      - No watermarks  
      - Advanced AI avatars  
      - Voice cloning  
      - Custom AI styles  

  - **CTA Button:**  
    Each card has a CTA button:  
    - **Free Plan:** "Get Started for Free"  
    - **Cat Mode:** "Upgrade to Cat Mode"  
    - **Super Cat:** "Go Super Cat"  

- **Feature Comparison Table:**  
  Below the plan cards, a detailed comparison table is provided, listing all features side by side. This table includes:  
  - **Video Export Quality** (720p, 1080p)  
  - **Credits per Month**  
  - **AI Voices and Avatars**  
  - **Voice Cloning**  
  - **Custom AI Styles**  
  - **Storage Limits**  
  - **Priority Support**  

  The table is interactive, allowing users to hover over features for tooltips explaining each one in more detail.
