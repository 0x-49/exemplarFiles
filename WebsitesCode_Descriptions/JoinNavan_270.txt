### Comprehensive Description of the Navan Connect Page

The **Navan Connect** page is a dedicated section of the Navan website designed to highlight the unique features and benefits of Navan Connect, a solution that allows businesses to integrate their existing corporate cards (Visa, Mastercard, or Amex) with the Navan platform. This page is structured to provide a seamless user experience, with clear, concise, and visually appealing elements that guide visitors through the value proposition, functionality, and benefits of Navan Connect. Below is a detailed breakdown of the page's structure, content, and design elements.

---

### **1. Hero Section**
The hero section is the first thing visitors see when they land on the Navan Connect page. It is designed to immediately capture attention and communicate the core value of Navan Connect.

- **Headline:**  
  *"Bring Your Own Cards. Simplify Expense Management."*  
  This headline is bold, concise, and directly addresses the primary benefit of Navan Connect: the ability to use existing corporate cards while streamlining expense management.

- **Subheadline:**  
  *"Link your Visa, Mastercard, or Amex corporate cards to Navan and automate expense reporting, approvals, and reconciliation—without changing your payment methods."*  
  This subheadline provides additional context, emphasizing the ease of integration and the automation of key processes.

- **Call-to-Action (CTA) Buttons:**  
  - *"Get Started"* (primary CTA, in a vibrant color like Navan’s signature blue)  
  - *"Watch a Demo"* (secondary CTA, in a neutral color like white or gray)  
  These buttons are prominently placed and encourage immediate engagement.

- **Visuals:**  
  A dynamic, high-quality image or animation showing a corporate card being seamlessly integrated into the Navan platform. The visual reinforces the concept of "bringing your own cards" and highlights the simplicity of the process.
