#### **2. Investment Calculator Tool**
The centerpiece of the page is the **Investment Calculator**, a powerful tool designed to help users forecast the financial performance of their self-storage investments. The calculator is interactive, user-friendly, and visually engaging.

- **Input Fields**:
  - Users can input key variables to customize their calculations:
    - **Facility Size**: Total number of units or square footage.
    - **Occupancy Rate**: Current or projected occupancy percentage.
    - **Rental Rates**: Average monthly rental income per unit.
    - **Operating Costs**: Monthly expenses such as utilities, staffing, and maintenance.
    - **Capital Expenditures**: Upfront costs for facility setup or upgrades.
    - **Loan Details**: Interest rates, loan terms, and down payment amounts (if applicable).
  - Each input field includes a tooltip or hover explanation to guide users on what to enter.

- **Dynamic Results**:
  - As users input data, the calculator dynamically updates the results in real-time.
  - Key metrics displayed include:
    - **Annual Revenue**: Projected income based on inputs.
    - **Net Profit**: Revenue minus operating costs and loan payments.
    - **Return on Investment (ROI)**: Percentage return on the initial investment.
    - **Break-Even Point**: The time required to recoup the initial investment.
  - Results are presented in both numerical and graphical formats, such as bar charts, pie charts, and line graphs.

- **Scenario Comparison**:
  - Users can save multiple scenarios (e.g., "Best Case," "Worst Case," "Current Plan") and compare them side-by-side.
  - A toggle switch allows users to view results on a monthly, quarterly, or annual basis.

- **Export Options**:
  - Users can export their calculations as a PDF or CSV file for further analysis or sharing with stakeholders.
