### Full Text Description of the VOC AI Demo Page

The VOC AI Demo page is designed to be a comprehensive, visually engaging, and interactive experience that showcases the platform's capabilities in sentiment analysis, customer feedback interpretation, and actionable insights. Below is a detailed description of all text elements, features, themes, colors, components, and functionalities that will be present on the page.

---

### **1. Hero Section**
**Purpose:** To immediately capture the user's attention and communicate the core value proposition of VOC AI.

- **Headline:**  
  *"Unlock the Power of Customer Insights with AI"*  
  - **Typography:** Bold, large font size (4xl-5xl) with a gradient or accent color (e.g., blue) for emphasis.  
  - **Theme:** Empowering and forward-thinking, emphasizing the transformative potential of AI.  

- **Subheadline:**  
  *"Understand what your customers truly feel and make data-driven decisions. Our AI-powered sentiment analysis goes beyond simple keywords to reveal the genuine emotions behind customer feedback."*  
  - **Typography:** Medium-sized font (lg-xl), neutral color (gray-700/dark:gray-300) for readability.  
  - **Theme:** Informative and persuasive, highlighting the emotional depth of the analysis.  

- **Call-to-Action (CTA) Button:**  
  *"Start Your Free Trial"*  
  - **Design:** Large, prominent button with a primary color (e.g., blue) and hover effects.  
  - **Functionality:** Redirects users to the sign-up or free trial page.  

- **Background:**  
  - Light gray (bg-gray-100) or dark mode (bg-gray-800) to ensure the text stands out.
